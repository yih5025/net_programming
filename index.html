Our Research Interest are
In the area of building ubiquitous computing, IoT, AI-enabled
applications in everyday lives to make our world a better place.

Specifically, we are interested in solving any research problem across
all domains from building data collection apparatus and collecting

Data sets from humans and their surroundings to analyzing
the collected data and building intelligent applications based on Machine
learning/deep learning techniques. Let's build the 'AI-everywhere' world together!
Techniques
are required for study in Ubicomplab at SCH, including sensors, open hardware boards,
programming languages, platforms, software libraries for learning systems.
